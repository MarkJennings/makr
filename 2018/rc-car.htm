RC Car


In my machine elements class, we had a project to design an RC Car, optimizing the drive train and steering with concepts we learned in the class. My team ended up getting 1st place out of 30+ teams!

The 3-wheeled design is to prevent rear wheel slippage which can result in unstable steering and wasted energy. Normal cars have a differential to prevent this, but that's difficult to accomplish on a small scale and limited budget.
